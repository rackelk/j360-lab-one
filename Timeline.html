Timeline

Due to his undying efforts to keep his personal information private, he does slip up a few times and reveal his secrets. It’s difficult to get the exact years of his life story, so this timeline is simply in chronological order. Ron Swanson’s life is a puzzle that we may never solve.

  • Ron Swanson is born to Tamara Swanson in a small town in Indiana
  • Age 9: Got his first job at a sheet metal factory at age nine and, in two weeks, was running the floor.
  • Age 15: Ron moves in with his first wife, referred to as Tammy One. Before their marriage she was his math and Sunday School teacher. They eventually divorce.
  • Age 18: Ron goes to college.
  • Year unknown: Ron marries another woman, referred to as Tammy Two. They, too, eventually divorce.
  • 2003:Ron starts job as Director of the Pawnee City Department of Parks and Recreation.
  • 2012: Ron falls in love with Diane. After an unplanned pregnancy, Ron proposes. Their marriage is still going strong.
  • 2017:Ron quits his government job and starts his own construction company, aptly named the Very Good Building Company.
