Biography

Ron Swanson is currently the owner of the Very Good Building Company, after leaving his position as the Director of the Pawnee City Department of Parks and Recreation. However, if it were up to him, the government would be privatized.

He is dedicated to protecting himself from the outside world. This includes staying completely off the internet and trying to hide all of his personal information. Despite his efforts to stay private, we do know some information about his life outside the office. Ron Swanson is an old soul. His secret alias, Duke Silver, is a very popular jazz musician.

Swanson embraces nature and all things manly. He’s set in his ways, and won’t change for anyone. He doesn’t talk often, but he has no problem sharing his opinions. Salad, according to Swanson, is rabbit food, and dogs that weigh less than 30 pounds are cats.
